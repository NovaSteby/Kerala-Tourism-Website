ERNAKULAM

Ernakulam, the commercial capital of Kerala, is a bustling urban center located on the coast of the Arabian Sea. Boasting a rich history intertwined with its maritime trade routes, Ernakulam has evolved into a vibrant metropolis known for its dynamic business environment and cosmopolitan culture. The city's skyline is dotted with modern skyscrapers, juxtaposed against remnants of its colonial past, such as the historic Mattancherry Palace and the iconic Fort Kochi area, where Dutch and Portuguese influences are still evident in its architecture and narrow streets..

Moreover, Ernakulam's strategic location as the gateway to the scenic backwaters of Kerala makes it a popular tourist destination. Visitors flock to the city to explore the tranquil backwaters, take leisurely boat rides along the picturesque Vembanad Lake, and immerse themselves in the serene ambiance of nearby hill stations like Munnar and Thekkady. With its bustling markets, vibrant cultural scene, and strategic location as a hub for both business and leisure travelers, Ernakulam remains a dynamic and alluring destination on Kerala's coastline.
