KANNUR

Kannur, nestled on the picturesque Malabar Coast of Kerala, is a captivating blend of rich history, cultural heritage, and natural beauty. Known for its pristine beaches, including the iconic Payyambalam Beach and the serene Muzhappilangad Drive-in Beach, Kannur offers breathtaking sunsets and tranquil seaside retreats. The district is steeped in history, boasting ancient forts like St. Angelo's Fort and the imposing Thalassery Fort, which stand as testaments to its colonial past. Kannur is also famous for its vibrant Theyyam rituals, a traditional art form that mesmerizes spectators with its elaborate costumes and mesmerizing performances. With its lush green landscapes, warm hospitality, and vibrant cultural scene, Kannur invites travelers to immerse themselves in its rich tapestry of experiences.
